BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG is the full name of the big German super company, which the whole world knows by the abbreviation BMW. BMW was founded in 1917 in the midst of World War by a German named Franz Josef. This company has been producing aircraft engines for 5 consecutive years.

DODGE

Dodge is one of the most well-known American car manufacturers, which is known for producing energetic and attractive cars. The history of this company dates back to 1900, but it was officially established under the name "Dodge" in 1914. Dodge cars are known for their unique and attractive designs, high energy and strong performance.

AUDI

The German automobile company is one of the famous manufacturers of luxury cars, which is headquartered in the city of Ingolstadt, Bavaria, and produces its models in 9 factories located in 8 European countries. Audi was founded on July 16, 1909 by August Horsch in Zwickau, Germany. Since 1964, this company is one of the subsidiary companies of Volkswagen Group.
